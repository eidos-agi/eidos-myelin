## Claim

A-Mem (NeurIPS 2025) creates Zettelkasten-inspired interconnected knowledge networks. When new memories are added, they trigger updates to existing memories — contextual representations and attributes continuously refine. Uses ChromaDB for vector storage. Superior to SOTA on 6 foundation models. Key difference: A-Mem operates within a session building a knowledge graph. Myelin distills across sessions into flat, scored tactics. A-Mem's linking approach could inform how myelin connects related tactics.
