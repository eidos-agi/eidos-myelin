## Claim

ExpeL framework (extending Reflexion) found that failure-derived heuristics substantially outperform success-derived ones — negative constraints that prune ineffective strategies are more valuable than positive patterns. For myelin: anti-patterns (type 2 knowledge) may be MORE valuable than tactics (type 1). The distillation pipeline should prioritize extracting 'what went wrong' over 'what went right'. Sessions with high tool-call/low-progress ratios are the richest source material.
