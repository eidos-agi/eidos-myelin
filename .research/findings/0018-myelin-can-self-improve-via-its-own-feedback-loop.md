## Claim

The tactic_feedback(id, worked) signal closes a recursive loop: myelin surfaces tactics → sessions produce outcomes → outcomes become data for improving distillation, retrieval, and thresholds. Four levels: (1) track activation vs rejection rates to learn what to distill, (2) track which surfaced tactics get acted on to improve retrieval, (3) calibrate promotion thresholds from accept/reject rates, (4) generate meta-tactics about myelin's own effectiveness. The evaluation harness (G3) isn't just measurement — it IS the self-improvement mechanism. This is the architectural moat vs static AGENTS.md files, which cannot observe their own impact.
